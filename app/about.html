What is TRASTRA

Trastra is a blockchain personal banking that combines benefits of Bitcoin and Ethereum with traditional payment infrastructure.

This is a payment card and fully-featured banking application.

In the application, you can easily buy and sell bitcoins and ethereum and convert it into traditional money to spend with a payment card issued by VISA.
